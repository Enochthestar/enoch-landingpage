The problem

Property managers waste hours
on things that should run themselves

Every day, property managers in Lagos, Abuja, and across West Africa lose money and time to these avoidable problems.

Missed calls = missed money
30–40% of tenant calls go unanswered during busy periods. Each missed request delays resolution and damages trust.
Scattered conversations
Requests come in through WhatsApp, calls, and walk-ins. Nothing is tracked. Things fall through the cracks constantly.
Hours chasing vendors
Calling plumbers, chasing electricians, following up on jobs — all done manually, over and over, every single day.
No paper trail
When disputes arise, there's no record of what was reported, when, or how it was resolved. It's your word against theirs.
Late rent collections
No automated reminders means tenants forget, managers chase, and cash flow suffers every month without fail.
Tenant dissatisfaction
Slow responses and zero communication updates drive good tenants away, increasing costly vacancy rates.
How it works

From request to resolution
in four simple steps

Step 01
Tenant submits request
Via a professional web form — on any phone, no app download needed. They describe the issue and upload a photo.
Step 02
You get an instant alert
Email notification lands in your inbox immediately with all details — name, unit, issue type, urgency, and photo.
Step 03
Dashboard logs everything
Every request is automatically saved to your dashboard. Sort by urgency, filter by property, track status in real time.
Step 04
Issue gets resolved
Assign vendor, update status to Resolved. Full history saved forever. No calls, no WhatsApp chaos, no lost requests.
Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built specifically for property managers in Nigeria and West Africa — not adapted from a Western tool.

Branded tenant form
Professional maintenance request form under your brand. Tenants submit from any phone — no login, no app, no friction.
